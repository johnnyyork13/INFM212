About Me

Hello! My name is Johnny York and I am currently a junior in Mercer's Software Application Development and Human-Computer Interaction program.

I completed my associate's degree in Computer Support at Georgia Northwestern Technical College in 2013 and have since been employed as a Communications Technician with one of the major cable providers.

At the beginning of last year I decided I wanted to finally pursue my passion in programming, so I began self-teaching through various bootcamps and online courses. Eventually, I decided to commit myself to completing my bachelor's degree which led me to Mercer University.
